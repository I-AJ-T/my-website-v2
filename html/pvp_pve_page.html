PVP and PVE

↓Overview↓

Seven Knights Idle Adventure features both PVP and PVE aspects that help you gain more resources to level up and summon heroes and pets.

PVP Aspects ⤵

  • Guilds
  • Arena
  • Elite Arena

PVP Aspects:

Go over guild, arena, and elite arena

PVE Aspects ⤵

  • Daily Dungeons
  • Special Dungeons
  • Infinite Tower

PVE Aspects:

Go over daily dungeons, special dungeons, and infinite tower

Additionally, there are events that players can participate in. Events are only available for a limited time, so be sure to complete them while they are available. The events are another way of gaining resources to summon and increase the levels of heroes and pets.
