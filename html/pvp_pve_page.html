PVP and PVE

PVP and PVE Overview

Seven Knights Idle Adventure features both PVP and PVE aspects that help players gain more resources to level up and summon heroes and pets.

PVP Aspects:

PVP Aspects ⤵

  • Guilds
    • Allows players to form groups and band together
    • Extra resources ad bonus stats can be obatin from being in a guild
    • Players can participate in "Boss Battle" and "Guild War"
  • Arena
    • Allows players to put their best team of 10 heroes against others
    • Players will compete for a rank and the higher the rank the better the rewards are
    • Can also purchase consumables from the Aena Shop using Gladiator's Tokens
      • Gladiator's Tokens are obtain from competing in arena battles
  • Elite Arena
    • Allows players to put their best 3 teams of 10 heroes against others
    • Players will compete for a rank and the higher the rank the better the rewards are
    • Can also purchase consumables from the Aena Shop using Commander's Tokens
      • Commander's Tokens are obtain from competing in arena battles

PVE Aspects:

PVE Aspects ⤵

  • Daily Dungeons
    • There are 5 daily dungeons that players explore for resources
      • The resources obtained are Gold, Hero EXP, Knight's Tokens, Relic Shards, and Pet EXP
  • Special Dungeons
    • Chaos Ruins puts 3 teams of 10 against one boss
      • The higher your score the better the rewards
    • Equipment Dungeon puts a team of 10 against one boss
      • The higher your tier the better the rewards
  • Towers
    • The higher you go in these the better the rewards
      • Infinite Tower
      • Mirage Tower
      • Tower of Ascension

Limited Time Events:

Additionally, there are events that players can participate in. Events are only available for a limited time, so be sure to complete them while they are available. The events are another way of gaining resources to summon and increase the levels of heroes and pets. They also are used for collaborations to obtain special heroes from things like anime.
