Checklist and Shop Overview

About the Checklist Page:

The Checklist page is another way of getting more resources.

The Checklist page contains ⤵

  • The Secret Basement Tab
    • Daily Dishes
      • Gives players food that grants stat bonuses
    • Pet Ranch
      • Gives players Star Shards to raise Blessing stats
    • Elke's Caildron
      • Allows players to craft certain resources and foods
    • Library
      • Is an archive of the places players have been and of the story conversations between characters
  • The Missions Tab
    • Contains Daily, Weekly, and Repeated Missions to complete
  • The Conquest Requests Tab
    • Contains a mini game to gain Dice used to raise hero stats
  • The Mine
    • Gives players the chance of gaining various amounts of Rainbow Ores used to level up hero accessories

About the Shop Page:

The Shop page is another way of getting more resources using in-game currencies or real money.

The Shop page contains ⤵

  • The Summons Tab
    • Here players can summon heroes by using the appropriate tickets or gems as currency.However, High Lords cannot be summoned here. They are obtained through the Shakles of Destiny
  • The Shop Tab
    • Here players can spend real money or other currencies like gems to obtain verious resources
  • Daily Shop Tab
    • Here players spend real money to obtain various resources
  • Monthly Packs Tab
    • Here players spend real money to obtain a monthly subscription to have no ads and an increase in gold obtained
